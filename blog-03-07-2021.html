Project Cloud 03/07/2021

Time Log


This sprint I spent 2 hours for 2 studio meetings, 5.5 for leads meetings, 0.5 for checkins with my producer, 8 creating the hub level greybox, 4 refining that greybox and importing level props and textures, 12.5 meeting with level design programmers, 2 for enemy pod meetings, and 1 for giving feedback. This sprint I created moving platforms, investigated placing navmeshlinks at runtime for the enemies pod, got navmeshes to be built and the level to be properly generated in a build, got temporary dungeon objective enemy wave spawning implemented, implemented dungeon objective keys, made optimizations to the level generator code, added temp visual distinctions to the enemies, implemented an enemy spawn rate system, fixed a bug related to that spawn system, added locks to moving platforms that need to be activated by a button prefab I made, and imported and setup the materials for the diesel parts pack we purchased, added working dungeon entrances to the hub level, added dungeon exit to end of dungeon objective, among other contributions.

Total 35.5 hours / 24 hours
