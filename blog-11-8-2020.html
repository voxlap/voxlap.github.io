Project Rogue 11/8/2020

Time Log

This sprint I spent 2 hours for 2 studio meetings, 2 for leads, 1 for checkins with my producer, 2 for giving feedback, and the rest of my time went to picking up tasks that members were unable to do because of finals, or to importing assets into the unity project. This sprint I created sprites for the scorpion, bandit, and sniper enemies and rigged them, as well as rigging the boss sprite Amber made. I also swapped out all of the sprites in the enemy prefabs to have the final art, and setup the references correctly in various scripts. I created a spark vfx and a death animation for the bandit which I made into a prefab. I created a level exit portal with vfx and a custom shadergraph. I imported the deset tileset, spliced the image into different sprites, created tiles for those, and made a tile pallette. I also created the base vectorized tundra tileset that Lisa then worked off of, and I imported her tileset into the game. For both tilesets, I setup the scriptable object for the level generators to use them correctly. I also went through and swapped out the weapon art for most of the weapons in game that have finished art.

Total 24 hours / 24 hours
