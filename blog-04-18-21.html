Project Retrospective

Caduceus has probably been the most difficult project I've worked on since Io in the winter semester of 2020. It started out largely over scoped, in an art style that didn't mesh well with our talent, with overly ambitious features. This meant that many, many systems got cut, many pieces of art that were created weren't up to par with the assets we ended up purchasing, and overall it has probably one of the least consistent art styles of any studio game thus far. Overall though, I'm happy with the final project. For how large of an undertaking this was, we got a lot more done than I thought that we would, and the final product is still enjoyable. I'm also sure that once I the showcase and look back on things, this project won't seem nearly as unpolished as it does now. Many lessons were learnt throughout this development cycle, and I hope that the studio will grow from them to make the next project the best that it can be!
